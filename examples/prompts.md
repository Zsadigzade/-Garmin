# Example Prompts

Use these prompts after connecting **GarminBud** in your MCP client.

## Activities

1. What did I do today?
2. Show me all my runs from last week.
3. Summarize my most recent workout.
4. How far did I run in the last 7 days?

## Sleep

5. How's my sleep been this week?
6. What was my sleep score last night?
7. Am I getting enough deep sleep lately?

## Heart Rate

8. Is my resting heart rate trending better?
9. What was my resting heart rate over the last 30 days?

## Recovery

10. Am I recovered enough to train hard today?
11. Should I take an easy day based on my recovery score?

## Body Composition

12. Am I losing fat or muscle over the last month?
13. What's my current weight trend?
